CashFlow App

Welcome! CashFlow App is a simple tool designed to map out your expected income and expenses over the course of a month. Upload a formatted Excel spreadsheet with income and expenses by date, and the app will display a cash flow chart along with a projected daily balance. This tool is meant to help visualize your cash flow over the course of a month to ensure liquidity at all times.

Fill in your income under the section titled "credits," and expenses under "debits." The "item" column is a description of the line item; in the "day" column specifiy the day of the month; and fill in the dollar value in the "amount" column. There is no limit to the number of line items. When complete, upload the spreadsheet below.
